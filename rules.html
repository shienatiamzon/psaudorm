PSAU Ladies Dorm Rules And Regulations

INTRODUCTION

Pampanga State Agricultural University provides domiciliary services to its students through the dormitory system. In the dormitories, the student lives in a home-simulated environment.

I. ADMISSION

  • File a reservation with the dorm master/matron upon enrollment.
  • Failing to report within five days forfeits the reservation.
  • Applicants must agree to all dorm rules, current and future.
  • Priority is given to freshmen and students with special needs.
  • Transfers or withdrawals during the semester require approval.

II. WITHDRAWAL RULES

Infractions will be dealt with under the Code of Student Discipline and may result in ejection from the dormitory.

III. DORMITORY NORMS

  • Foster harmony, cooperation, and decency.
  • No smoking, drinking, or gambling.
  • Report illnesses immediately to staff.
  • All students must be in by 7:00 PM (unless given permission).

IV. DORMITORY FACILITIES

  • Use dorm furniture and facilities properly.
  • No unauthorized appliance connections or tampering.
  • Damaged items must be replaced by the responsible resident.

V. CLEANING

  • Residents are responsible for room and common area cleanliness.
  • No animals allowed (case-by-case exceptions).
  • Garbage must be disposed of daily (5:00 AM – 1:00 PM).
  • Laundry should only be hung in designated areas.
  • Room inspections occur daily by dorm staff.
  • Mass cleaning is held twice a week.

VI. VISITORS

  • All visitors must sign the logbook.
  • Visiting hours:
    • Mon–Fri: 6:00 PM – 7:30 PM
    • Sat–Sun: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Php50 fee for approved overnight guests (emergency only).

VII. CURFEW HOURS

  • Curfew: 7:00 PM for ladies, 9:00 PM for men.
  • Study silence: 9:00 PM – 4:00 AM.
  • Late arrivals without permission will be marked delinquent.
  • Unauthorized fence entry/exit is prohibited.

VIII. OTHERS

  • Weekend whereabouts must be logged.
  • Students must take belongings home for long vacations.
  • Permission is needed to stay during breaks.

IX. PENALTY

Violations are subject to penalties under the Student Code of Conduct and Discipline.
