Help Center – PSAU Ladies Dorm Website

Student COR Not Yet Available

• Submit a temporary document as proof of enrollment at PSAU (e.g., screenshot of enrollment status, temporary slip, or official confirmation).

• Ensure it includes your name, student number, and academic term.

• Submit to the dormitory office until your official COR is available.

Error While Using the Website

• Take note of the error or screenshot it.

• Email psaudorm@example.com with a detailed description of the issue.

• We’ll investigate and respond as soon as possible.

Submitted Incorrect Information

• Email psaudorm@example.com immediately.

• Include your full name, student ID, and the correct information.

• Avoid submitting multiple forms to prevent delays.

Maintenance Request or Room Issue

• If you encounter a maintenance issue in your dorm room (e.g., broken fixtures, plumbing problems, etc.):

• Report the issue immediately to psaudorm@example.com

• Include your room number, full name, student ID, and a detailed description of the problem.

• Our maintenance team will address the issue as quickly as possible.

Delayed Payment

• Inform the dormitory admin as soon as possible, especially if it affects your reservation.

• If payment has been made but not reflected, send the receipt or transaction proof to psaudorm@example.com.

• For financial concerns, inquire about payment arrangements or assistance.

• Stay in communication to avoid penalties or slot cancellation.

Important Reminder After Booking

• After successfully booking a room, students are required to read the full Rules and Regulations of the PSAU Ladies Dormitory.

• These guidelines ensure a safe, respectful, and smooth stay for all boarders.

• You may access the Rules and Regulations through the booking confirmation page or request a copy from the dormitory office.
