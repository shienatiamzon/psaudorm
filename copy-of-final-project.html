About Us

History

This edifice known as the Ladies Dormitory was totally renovated during the incumbency of Dr. Honorio M. Soriano Jr., University President, using government funds through the sponsorship of Congressman Carmelo " Jon " B. Lazatin II to provide conducive living quarters for out-of-town students.

Formerly reopened on January 28, 2019 during 4th founding anniversary of Pampanga State Agricultural University.

What we Offer

We offer spacious rooms good for 8 people (4 double-deck beds), each with its own bathroom. The rates are very affordable, and the dorm is located inside the PSAU campus making it super convenient and hassle-free for students.

Inquiries are accepted only after you get your Certificate of Registration (COR) during enrollment. First-year students are given priority since they are just starting their journey and need more support and guidance.
